Enter The Dragon

Because everyone deserves their own walk-on music

Sign up to be notified when the Mac app is released

Based on a hack created by Francisco Rossi and Joseph Goldbeck at Music Hack Day Boston, Enter the Dragon uses bluetooth signal strength to detect when a user enters the room and plays their personalized entrance music. Features for v1.0 of the Mac app are still being determined, but we plan to allow the choice of specific songs and playlists via iTunes and Rdio. The app will be free for up to 2 users, and with the option of an in-app purchase to allow more.
