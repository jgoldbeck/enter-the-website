Fanfare

In today's world, everyone deserves a spectacular entrance

We intend to give it to them. Sign up to be notified when the Mac app is released

Based on a hack created by Francesco Rossi and Joseph Goldbeck at Music Hack Day Boston, Fanfare detects when a user enters the room using bluetooth signal strength and plays their personalized entrance music - finally giving them the welcome they deserve. Features for v1.0 of the Mac app are still being determined, but we plan to allow the choice of songs and playlists via iTunes and Rdio, as well as the ability to choose what times of day a grand entrance is desired (arriving at work in the morning, returning from lunch break, coming home after a long day at the space mines).
