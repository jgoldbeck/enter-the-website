Ingresso

Because everyone deserves their own entrance music

Sign up to be notified when the Mac app is released

Based on a hack created by Francesco Rossi and Joseph Goldbeck at Music Hack Day Boston, Ingresso uses bluetooth signal strength to detect when a user enters the room and plays their personalized entrance music. Features for v1.0 of the Mac app are still being determined, but we plan to allow the choice of songs and playlists via iTunes and Rdio, as well as the ability to choose what times of day a grand entrance is desired (arriving at work in the morning, returning from lunch break, coming home after a long day at the space mines). The app will be free for up to 2 users, with the option of an in-app purchase to allow more.
