Fanfare

In today's world, everyone deserves a spectacular entrance

We intend to give it to them. Sign up to be notified when the Mac app is released

Based on a hack created by Francesco Rossi and Joseph Goldbeck at Music Hack Day Boston, Fanfare detects when a user enters the room using bluetooth signal strength and plays their personalized entrance music - finally giving them the welcome they deserve. Version 1.0 of the Mac app is coming soon, so sign up for the mailing list and stay tuned...
